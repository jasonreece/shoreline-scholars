*A fun, interactive beginner class where kids build confidence, learn practical skills, and explore a new language and culture.*

Spanish Level 1 is a welcoming first step into a new language. Over eight weeks, students ages 10 and up learn to speak, listen, and connect in Spanish through games, stories, and real-life conversation. It's designed to feel less like a lesson and more like an invitation — a relaxed, encouraging space where kids build confidence one word at a time.

Language is a doorway to culture, and this class opens it wide. Students don't just memorize vocabulary; they explore the traditions, stories, and everyday rhythms that make Spanish come alive — and they leave each session a little more curious about the world beyond the shoreline.

## What Students Will Experience

- Fun, interactive lessons that keep kids engaged
- Real-life conversation and practical speaking skills
- Cultural exploration woven through every class
- Games, stories, and hands-on activities
- A supportive environment that builds confidence
