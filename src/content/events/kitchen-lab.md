A hands-on cooking experience for kids — learn to prepare Asian cuisine from scratch in a fun, supportive kitchen environment.

## What We're Making

- **Vietnamese Spring Rolls** — fresh rice paper rolls filled with vegetables, herbs, and protein
- **Korean Kimbap** — sushi-style rolls with seasoned rice, vegetables, and fillings

## What Students Will Learn

- Knife safety
- Food preparation skills
- Rolling techniques
- Flavor combinations from different cultures
- Kitchen teamwork
