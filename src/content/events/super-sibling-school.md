Welcoming a new baby is one of the biggest transitions a family can go through — and it's just as big for the older sibling. This class is designed to prepare children for that moment with confidence, excitement, and care.

## What Your Child Will Learn

- What new babies are really like
- How babies eat and grow
- How to safely hold and interact with a baby
- Techniques to support connection within the family
- How to manage big feelings like jealousy and anxiety

## What Families Receive

- A fun, memorable experience for your child
- Take-home materials for parents
- Take-home and in-class activities for siblings
- Practical strategies to support the transition at home
