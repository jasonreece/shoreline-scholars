What does your town government actually do? How are local laws made? What happens inside Town Hall, the police station, and the fire station?

This summer, Shoreline Scholars is partnering with Councilman John Brady to bring civics to life for students and families in Indialantic.

## What Students Will Learn

- How local government works
- The history of our town
- What ordinances are and how they're created

## Facility Tours

Students and families will get hands-on tours of three civic landmarks:

- **Town Hall** — See where local decisions are made
- **Police Station** — Meet officers and learn about community safety
- **Fire Station** — Explore the trucks and equipment up close
