Keep the learning going this summer — outside, at the beach, the way it should be.

Summer Review & Activities brings together academic review and outdoor play in a relaxed, small-group setting at Nance Park. Every Thursday morning, students will sharpen core skills, get creative, and stay active right by the shore.

## What's Included

- Review of main subjects with included workbooks
- Art projects on the beach
- Volleyball, kickball & board games
- Two small groups by age: 5–7 and 8–10

This is **not a drop-off program**. Parents are welcome to participate alongside their children or relax nearby at the park, beach, or pavilion while the group is in session.
