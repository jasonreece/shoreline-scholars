A hands-on workshop where storytelling meets art — students will write original stories and bring them to life with watercolor illustrations, all in a beautiful outdoor setting.

## What We'll Do

- **Creative Writing** — craft original short stories with guided prompts and coaching
- **Watercolor Illustration** — learn watercolor techniques to illustrate your story
- **Story Sharing** — read your finished piece aloud and celebrate each other's creativity

## What Students Will Learn

- Story structure and creative writing fundamentals
- Watercolor techniques for beginners
- How illustration and writing work together
- Building confidence in creative expression
- Giving and receiving encouragement in a group setting
